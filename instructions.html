About the game

Minesweeper is logical game, originaly introducen on Windows 7. It has long history, and is one of the best games out there to train your brain.


Objective

The objective of Minesweeper is to clear a minefield without detonating any hidden mines.


Minefield explained

1. The minefield is divided into a grid of cells. Each cell is either empty or contains a mine.
2. To begin the game, click on any cell to reveal its contents.
3. If the revealed cell contains a mine, the game is over and you have lost.
4. If the revealed cell is empty, it will display a number indicating the number of mines in the eight adjacent cells (up, down, left, right, and diagonals).
5. Use the numbers as clues to determine which cells are safe to reveal and which contain mines.
6. To mark a cell as containing a mine, right-click on it.
7. This will place a flag on the cell to indicate that you believe it contains a mine.


Did I win?

You win if you correctly flag all the mines, or if you reveal all the cells that do not contain mines.
